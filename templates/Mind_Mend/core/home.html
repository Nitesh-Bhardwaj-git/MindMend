Q&A FAQ

Answers to common questions about MindMend.

🧠 MindMend is an AI-powered mental health support platform combining a 24/7 AI chatbot, clinically validated self-assessments (PHQ-9, GAD-7, PSS-10), mood tracking, professional counsellor booking (regular & instant), live chat & video sessions, community forum, and a personalized analytics dashboard — all in one stigma-free space.

🤖 The AI chatbot uses sentiment analysis to read the emotional tone of your messages and generates personalized, empathetic responses. It remembers your past conversations — stress topics, preferred name, helpful activities — to provide continuity across sessions. It's available 24/7 and supports voice input so you can speak instead of type.

🧠 Yes. MindMend stores a lightweight long-term memory of your chat history — including stress topics, coping activities you've found helpful, your last emotional context, and your preferred name — so the AI provides more personalized and context-aware support every time you return.

✨ MindMend uniquely combines: AI emotional support with memory, evidence-based clinical assessments, daily mood tracking with energy & activity logs, a moderated community forum, professional counsellor booking (both scheduled & instant), live text chat & video sessions, Razorpay payments, real-time counsellor notifications, a personalized analytics dashboard with downloadable PDF reports, and a regional mental health heatmap — all in one platform.

🤝 MindMend uses AES-128/HMAC-SHA256 (Fernet) encryption for all sensitive conversations, HMAC-signed Razorpay payment verification with a server-side webhook fallback, OTP-based email verification on registration, verified professional counsellors with displayed qualifications, and strict access controls so only the right account sees each session's data.

💬 Yes. The AI chatbot and the Helplines page are accessible without logging in. However, creating a free account unlocks personalized memory, saved chat history, mood tracking, assessments, booking, dashboard analytics, and forum posting.

🔒 Yes. AI chat history and counsellor chat messages are encrypted at rest using AES-128/HMAC-SHA256 (Fernet encryption). Your account data, assessment results, and mood logs are kept strictly private inside your MindMend profile.

🗑️ Yes. Go to My Profile to permanently delete either your saved data only or your full account. A confirmation warning is shown before deletion because this action is irreversible.

🔐 No. MindMend does not sell your mental health data. Counsellors only see information tied to their own booked sessions — your assessment scores, mood logs, and AI chat history are never visible to them.

⚙️ You can: post anonymously in the community forum, hide your real name behind your username, book counsellor sessions anonymously (the counsellor sees "Anonymous Patient"), and opt out of location tracking anytime from your profile settings.

📍 Yes. Location sharing is entirely optional and can be declined at the banner prompt or turned off later in your profile. All core features — AI chat, assessments, mood tracker, booking, forum — work fully without sharing GPS coordinates.

✉️ After signing up, MindMend sends a 6-digit OTP to your email. You must verify it within 15 minutes before your account is activated. For password resets, a separate OTP flow keeps your account secure without exposing your password.

🛡️ Your data is protected with encrypted storage for sensitive conversations, OTP-based email verification, HMAC-SHA256 signed Razorpay payments (plus server-side webhook reliability), and strict per-session access controls so no other account can view your data.

👥 Anyone — students, working professionals, or anyone seeking mental health support and emotional well-being. MindMend is designed to be accessible, stigma-free, and welcoming to all.
