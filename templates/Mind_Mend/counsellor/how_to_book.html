Steps

{% for label in "Choose Counsellor,Pick a Date,Time Slot,Session Format,Notes & Privacy,Confirm & Pay,Attend Session,Complete & Review"|split:"," %} {{ forloop.counter }} {{ label }} {% endfor %}
1

Choose a Counsellor

Browse available counsellors on the booking page. Each card shows their specialization, star rating, years of experience, and session fee.

💡

Read each counsellor's bio and specialization to find the right match for your needs.

2

Pick a Date

Use the date picker to choose a day within the counsellor's available days (shown on their card — e.g., Mon, Wed, Fri).

📅

For Instant Booking, the date is locked to today — no picker needed.

3

Select a Time Slot

The time slot grid shows all available 30-minute windows within the counsellor's working hours.

⏱ Each session is exactly 30 minutes. Strikethrough = already booked.

4

Choose Session Format

Select one or both session formats. At least one must be selected to proceed.

💬

Chat Session

Live real-time encrypted text messaging — accessible from My Bookings and inside the video room simultaneously.

📹

Video Calling

Private video room with mic/camera toggles, 30-minute countdown, picture-in-picture, and integrated chat sidebar.

💡

Each format has its own fee. Selecting both adds the fees. Total shown before you confirm.

5

Add Notes & Choose Anonymity

Optionally share a brief note with your counsellor. You can also check Keep my identity hidden — the counsellor sees your booking as "Anonymous Patient" instead of your name.

🕶️

Anonymity applies to the counsellor's view only. Your booking stays linked to your account for payment and chat.

6

Confirm & Pay

Click Book Appointment. If there's a fee, you'll be redirected to secure Razorpay checkout. After payment, your booking is Confirmed instantly. Free sessions skip payment.

🔒

Payments are 100% secure, processed by Razorpay.

🔔

Counsellor notified in real time on confirmation.

7

Attend Your Session

Go to My Bookings to view your confirmed appointment. From your booking card, you can:

  • 💬Open Chat to message your counsellor before, during, or after the session — all messages encrypted.
  • 📹Click Join Video (if Video was selected) to enter the private video room with mic/camera controls, 30-minute timer, and chat sidebar.
  • ⚡For Instant Bookings, you're redirected to chat or video immediately after payment — no waiting.
8

Complete & Leave a Review

Either party can mark the session as Completed — or it auto-completes 24 hours after the scheduled time.

  • ⭐Leave a 1–5 star rating and a written review to help others choose the right counsellor.
  • 🗑️Optionally delete the session record — this removes the booking and all associated chat history permanently.
  • 💾If you don't delete, the record stays in My Bookings so you can reference the chat history anytime.
