How to Book a Session

Follow these simple steps to schedule a private, secure session with one of our certified counsellors — via text chat, video call, or both.

📅

Standard

Regular Booking

Pick a future date and 30-minute slot with any certified counsellor. Best for planned, unhurried sessions.

Book Now →
⚡

Instant

Instant Booking

Connect with an on-duty counsellor today, right now. Higher fees than regular, but no waiting required.

Connect Now →
1

Choose a Counsellor

Browse the list of available counsellors on the right side of the booking page. Each card shows their specialization, star rating, years of experience, and session fee. Click the → button to view their full profile — verified qualifications, bio, medical registration, and consultation policy.

💡

Read each counsellor's bio and specialization to find the right match for your needs.

2

Pick a Date

Use the date picker to choose a day within the counsellor's available days (shown on their card — e.g., Mon, Wed, Fri). Choosing an unavailable day will display a validation error with the correct days listed.

📅

For Instant Booking, the date is locked to today — no picker needed. Just select a counsellor who has Instant enabled and go.

3

Select a Time Slot

After picking a counsellor and date, the time slot grid appears, showing all available 30-minute windows within the counsellor's working hours.

4

Choose Session Format

Select one or both session formats. At least one must be selected to proceed.

💬

Chat Session

Live real-time encrypted text messaging with your counsellor — accessible from My Bookings and also inside the video call room simultaneously.

📹

Video Calling

A private video room with your live camera (picture-in-picture), mic & camera toggle controls, a 30-minute countdown timer, and an integrated chat sidebar.

💡

Each format has its own fee set by the counsellor. Selecting both adds the fees together. The total is shown before you confirm.

5

Add Notes & Choose Anonymity

Optionally share a brief note with your counsellor (what you'd like to discuss, any concerns going in). You can also check Keep my identity hidden — if selected, the counsellor sees your booking as "Anonymous Patient" instead of your name or username.

🕶️

Anonymity applies to the counsellor's view only. Your booking is still linked to your account for payment and chat.

6

Confirm & Pay

Click Book Appointment. If the session has a fee, you'll be redirected to the secure Razorpay payment page. After successful payment, your booking is Confirmed instantly. Free sessions skip payment and are confirmed immediately.

🔒

Payments are 100% secure and processed by Razorpay.

🔔

Your counsellor is notified in real time when your booking is confirmed.

7

Attend Your Session

Go to My Bookings to view your confirmed appointment. From your booking card, you can:

  • 💬 Open the Chat button to message your counsellor before, during, or after the session. All messages are encrypted end-to-end.
  • 📹 Click Join Video (if Video Calling was selected) to enter the private video room with mic/camera controls, a 30-minute timer, and a built-in chat sidebar.
  • ⚡ For Instant Bookings, you are redirected to the chat or video room immediately after payment confirmation — no waiting needed.
8

Complete & Leave a Review

Either you or the counsellor can mark the session as Completed — or it auto-completes 24 hours after the scheduled time. After completion:

  • ⭐ Leave a 1–5 star rating and a written review to help others choose the right counsellor.
  • 🗑️ Optionally delete the session record from My Bookings — this removes the booking and all associated chat history permanently.
  • 💾 If you don't delete, the record stays in My Bookings so you can reference the chat history anytime.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Regular and Instant booking? ▾

Regular Booking lets you schedule a 30-minute session on any future date and available time slot. You choose your counsellor, date, time, and session format at your own pace. Instant Booking connects you with an on-duty counsellor for today with no date selection — perfect for urgent support. Instant fees are typically higher than regular session fees.

Can I cancel my booking? ▾

Yes — as long as you haven't completed payment, you can cancel from the cancel option on your booking card in My Bookings. Cancellation frees up the slot for other users. Unpaid pending bookings are also auto-cleaned after 15 minutes.

Why is my preferred time slot greyed out? ▾

Greyed-out (strikethrough) slots are already booked by another user. Each session lasts 30 minutes, so the next available slot is always 30 minutes after the last booked one.

How do I access the Video Call room? ▾

After your booking is confirmed, go to My Bookings and click Join Video on your booking card. The video room features: your live camera (picture-in-picture), a simulated counsellor feed, mic/camera toggle controls, a 30-minute countdown timer, and a side chat panel for real-time messaging.

Can I book a session anonymously? ▾

Yes. Check Keep my identity hidden when booking — the counsellor will see "Anonymous Patient" in their dashboard instead of your name. Your booking remains linked to your account for payment and chat access.

Can I book multiple sessions? ▾

Yes! You can book multiple sessions with the same or different counsellors, as long as the time slots don't overlap. Each booking appears separately in My Bookings.

What happens after a session ends? ▾

Either you or the counsellor can mark the session as Completed, or it auto-completes 24 hours after the scheduled time. After that, you can leave a star review and optionally delete the session record (which removes all chat history). If you want to keep the chat, simply don't delete it — it stays visible under My Bookings.

Will my counsellor be notified when I book or send a message? ▾

Yes. Counsellors receive real-time notifications for new bookings, incoming chat messages, and session status changes through their doctor dashboard — delivered instantly via WebSocket when online, and stored persistently so they don't miss anything when offline.
