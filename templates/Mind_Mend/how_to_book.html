How to Book a Session

Follow these simple steps to schedule a private, secure session with one of our certified counsellors.

1

Choose a Counsellor

Browse the list of available counsellors on the right side of the booking page. Each card shows their specialization, rating and session fee. Select the counsellor that best fits your needs from the dropdown in the form.

💡

Read each counsellor's bio and specialization to find the right match for you.

2

Pick a Date

Use the date picker to choose a day that falls within the counsellor's available days (shown on their card). Choosing an unavailable day will show an error — the form will tell you which days work.

📅

Always book at least a day in advance so your counsellor has time to prepare.

3

Select a Time Slot

After picking a counsellor and date, the time slot grid will appear, showing all available 30-minute windows within the counsellor's working hours.

4

Add Notes (Optional)

You can share a brief note with your counsellor — for example, what you'd like to discuss or any concerns you have going in. This is entirely optional but helps your counsellor prepare.

5

Confirm & Pay

Click Book Appointment. If the counsellor charges a session fee, you'll be taken to the secure Razorpay payment page. After successful payment, your booking is Confirmed instantly.

🔒

Payments are 100% secure and processed by Razorpay.

6

Attend Your Session & Chat

Go to My Bookings to view your confirmed appointment. Use the 💬 Chat button to communicate with your counsellor before, during, or after the session. Once the session ends, you can leave a review.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my booking? ▾

Yes — as long as you haven't completed payment, you can cancel from the three-dot menu (⋮) on your booking card in My Bookings. Cancellation frees up the slot for other users.

Why is my preferred time slot greyed out? ▾

Greyed-out (strikethrough) slots are already booked by another user. Each session lasts 30 minutes, so the next available slot is always 30 minutes after the last booked one.

Can I book multiple sessions? ▾

Yes! You can book multiple sessions with the same or different counsellors, as long as the time slots don't overlap. Each booking appears separately in My Bookings.

What happens after a session ends? ▾

Either you or the counsellor can mark the session as Completed. After that, you can leave a star review and optionally delete the session record (which removes all chat history). If you want to keep the chat, simply don't delete it — it stays visible under My Bookings.
