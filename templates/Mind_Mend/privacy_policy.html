🛡️ PRIVACY & SECURITY

Privacy Policy

Your mental health data is personal. This policy explains exactly what we collect, how we protect it, and the controls you have.

Last updated: April 2026

🔐

Encrypted Messaging

All counsellor chat messages and AI chatbot conversations are encrypted at rest using AES-128 (Fernet / HMAC-SHA256). Messages stored in our database are unreadable without the private encryption key. All traffic is encrypted in transit over HTTPS.

1

What we collect

Account information

Username, email address, and optional profile details (first/last name, date of birth, gender, occupation). Your real name is never displayed publicly unless you explicitly enable it.

Mental health data

Mood entries, assessment responses (PHQ-9, GAD-7, PSS), and AI chatbot conversations. This data is tied to your account and never shared with third parties.

Location data (optional)

Approximate region (country, state, city) derived from your IP address or GPS (if you consent). Used only for aggregate analytics. You can opt out any time in your profile settings.

Session & cookies

A session cookie to keep you logged in, and a CSRF cookie that protects against cross-site attacks. No third-party tracking cookies are set without your consent.

2

How we protect your data

✓

Encryption at rest — counsellor chat messages and AI chatbot history are encrypted in the database using Fernet symmetric encryption (AES-128-CBC + HMAC-SHA256). Raw ciphertext is meaningless without the private key.

✓

Encryption in transit — all data is transmitted over HTTPS with HSTS headers enforced in production, preventing downgrade attacks.

✓

CSRF protection — every form and API request is protected against cross-site request forgery using Django's CSRF middleware.

✓

Password hashing — passwords are hashed using Django's PBKDF2-SHA256 algorithm and are never stored in plain text.

✓

Single-device sessions — logging in on a new device automatically invalidates all other active sessions for your account.

✓

Razorpay payment security — payments are processed by Razorpay with HMAC-SHA256 signature verification. We never store card details.

3

Your privacy controls

🎭

Anonymous posting

Forum posts and replies are anonymous by default. Your username is only shown if you opt in per post.

👤

Display name control

Your real name is hidden by default. Enable it only if you want it visible in chats and reviews.

📍

Location tracking opt-out

Go to My Profile → Privacy Settings and enable "Opt out of location tracking" to disable all geolocation logging.

🍪

Cookie consent

You can decline cookies on the consent banner. Declining disables geolocation sharing from your browser.

4

Data sharing & retention

✗

We do not sell your data to any third party.

✗

We do not share your mental health data with employers, insurers, or advertisers.

✓

Counsellors can only see messages within their own booked sessions. No cross-session access is possible.

✓

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us via the Contact page.

5

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Razorpay — payment processing (PCI-DSS compliant)
  • Google Gemini / OpenAI — AI chatbot (only your message text is sent, not your identity)
  • Google Analytics — aggregate page-view metrics (consent banner controls this)
  • Render.com — hosting infrastructure
